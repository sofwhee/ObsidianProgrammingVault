Start with a box of equal sides.
This will determine the angle of the body part.
It will also determine the perspective for the rest of your drawing.
## Drawing a box in perspective

**Finding the axis**
Draw an X on a face.
![[Pasted image 20260517094617.png]]

**Rotation**
Use an ellipse to rotate in perspective.
![[Pasted image 20260517094744.png]]

**Perspective**
Use vanishing points X, Y, and Z.
They don't need to perfectly touch at the vanishing point. Just get close.
![[Pasted image 20260517094857.png]]

If you rotate the box by the Y axis, you'll see the y axis stays in perspective but the others don't.
However, the X and Z do slide along the horizon line.
![[Pasted image 20260517094956.png]]![[Pasted image 20260517095407.png]]
