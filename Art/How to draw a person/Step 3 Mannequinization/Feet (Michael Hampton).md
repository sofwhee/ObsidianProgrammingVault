#### Heel
You can draw a coke can or cylinder.
This feels more organic to me and I prefer to use it.
Or you can use a box that you can project forward, which is easier.

#### MetaTarsis
People tend to draw bumps and stuff along this curve. Don't do that.
You're probably just seeing a vein or a tendon, don't bother.
![[Pasted image 20260518092625.png]]

Start from the corners of the tarsis.
Have it curve down like a slide.
When it hits the ground plane, have it change direction along it.
![[Pasted image 20260518092728.png]]

#### Toes
Use sticks and spheres, like the hand.
The four toes point towards the ground.
The big toe points toward the sky.
