Skin is thickest and toughest at the heel.
Thin and delicate in the middle of the sole.
Especially the medial (inner) side.

This means that skin folds are pretty calm around the heel, and pretty deep in the obvious places.

The fat pads at the end of the foot are there to protect the toes.
They cause the toes to look smaller from the bottom.
They also make it look like the toes start above the balls of the foot.
