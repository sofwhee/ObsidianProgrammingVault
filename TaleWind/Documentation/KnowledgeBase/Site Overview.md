#### My Account
- Non-admin Logged-in users also have access.
- Change personal info, subscriptions, purchase history
- Personal info:
	- Profile photo (desktop upload)
	- Required info:
		- First Name, Last Name, Email
		- Critical for identifying you when making changes and getting email notifs.
	- Optional info: (all plain text)
		- Organization, Professional Title
		- phone number
		- country, state/province, zip/postal code
		- Biography
- Subscriptions
	- Receive notifications when new content is uploaded to a channel
		- Check boxes beside channels and click Update Subscriptions
		- Click "select all" to check all boxes
		- To unsubscribe, deselect channels, or click Deselect All, then Update Subscriptions
- Purchase History
	- Requires eCommerice integration (contact your AM)
	- See all purchases you have made on the site
		- Order number, item, purchase date, price

#### Users

Instead of relying on a single person or dept, you can allow everyone to manage users.
- Manage users
	- All created users on your site
		- General
		- Super Admins, Admins
		- Editors, Authors, Contributors
	- View and edit user details
	- Add new users
	- more (?)
- Manage User Groups
	- User group: Segment admin users into smaller groups with their own content managers.
	- Content Managers: Editors can manage a group of Authors and Contributors
	- Allows admins/super admins to ensure content is being added, without being required to be experts on all content.
- Audience Roles
	- Tool for admins to set access restrictions for different groups of users.
	- Examples:
		- Audience role for users from a particular event, so they can access event recordings
		- Upload a member list and only allow members to access certain content

#### Ads

- Manage ads:
	- Banner ads, video ads, video annotations
	- Either as house ads or sponsor ads.
- Banner ads
	- Available on every site page
	- Upload banner files, manage placement, view analytics
	- Can be:
		- Sitewide
		- Page-specific
		- channel-specific
		- post-specific
- Video Ads
	- Available to be placed
	- Pre-roll or post-roll on any video
	- Upload video files, manage placements, view analytics
	- NOTE!, it says "view analytics for your **banners**" That should be **videos**.
- Annotations (for videos)
	- Interactive elements that overlay directly on the video player.
	- Viewers can click to open an assigned URL
	- Often used as calls to action at the end of a video.
	- Blank annotations - Create a clickable area without a visual. Commonly used when visual cue or button is already visually in the video.
	- Image annotations - Overlay a clickable image on the video in a defined position.
	- Text annotations - Overlay a clickable text on the video in a defined position.

#### Distribution

- Use the Distribution module to define your distribution strategy. Maximize the success of your media platform.
- Channel Management
	- You can segment your content in a meaningful way, so it's easier for viewers to find what they need.
- Tags Manager
	- See all the tags you have and adjust them.
	- Tags help improve searchability, as well as improving SEO for your content.
- Podcast Feeds
	- An add-on feature for the media platform
	- Helps keep your podcasts organized and updated across third party sites. (Spotify)
	- If you don't have podcast feeds enabled, you can Learn more from your AM
- Embed Codes
	- Lets you place content elsewhere in your site, and your partners' and sponsors' sites.
	- Embed a single video, or embed a smart player that shows the most recent video.
	- Many customizable features that can include different kinds of relevant info.

	- Top posts, top countries, top referrers

- Events (report) (live events hosted on site)

- Referrals (report)
