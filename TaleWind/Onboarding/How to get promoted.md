Yield

If you're available for everything, it makes you look quite junior.
The higher up you go, the less involved in the doing you are.
Pull your weight, but still act like the next level.
Don't ask "can I do this?"
What would someone in the next level do with this?
- Take it on?
- Delegate it?
- Challenge the value of doing it at all?
Don't try to look busy. Try to look *ready.*
Ready means showing you can make decisions.
- You can prioritize
- You can zoom out
- You can make trade offs

If you do all this well, then the next time the opportunity comes up, because
- You've been letting people know that you're trying to grow
- And you've been contributing in ways that leadership actually values
They will reach out to you.
