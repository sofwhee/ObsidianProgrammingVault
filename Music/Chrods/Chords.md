## Chord change

The transition from one chord to another.
The fundamental building block of harmony.
Defines the progression, mood, movement of a song.

Typically occurs at regular intervals, such as every measure or every two measures.

The shift in the underlying harmony.
Affecting the relationship between the bass note and upper notes.

##### Chord Change

The music moves from one chord to another.

##### Chord Progression

A sequence of chord changes.
Creates musical tension, release, and structure.
Some chords add lift (tension) other bring a sense of resolution.

###### Home base
- This is the primary chord. They typically revolve around this ("tonic" or "1 chord")
- Feels like "home" and resolves musical tension
###### Roman Numerals
- Uppercase = Major chord, lowercase = minor chord

1st - tonic
2nd - supertonic
3rd mediant
4th subdominant
5th dominant
6th submediant
7th subtonic or leading tone
