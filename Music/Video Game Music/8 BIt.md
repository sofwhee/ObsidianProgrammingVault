Square waves  - Main lead melody and counter melodies
Pulse wave - use a 12.5% or 25% pulse for thinner, punchier leads or bass
Triangle wave - Almost exclusively for the bassline. warm rounded subbass quality
White noise - Percussion. Modulate envelope to make quick kick drums, crisp hi hats and snares.

**Arpeggio** - Old consoles could only play 3 or 4 notes at a time. To create the illusion of a full chord, quickly alternate the notes of a chord back and forth

**A-B-A-C** Structure
- Break your 8 bar core melody into four sections
- Section A - main highly memorable hook
- Section B - Variation that adds tension
- Return to section A
- End with section C to introduce a new exciting element
