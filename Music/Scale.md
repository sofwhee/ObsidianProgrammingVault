Acts as a framework for musical composition.
Establish a "Key" / "Tonality"
"Happy" major scale
"Sad" minor scale

Every scale is defined by its unique pattern of intervals (steps) between notes
- whole-tone step
- half-tone step

**Structure**
- runs from a root up to its octave
- can have 5 notes (pentatonic), 7 (diatonic), 12 (chromatic)

**Raising / Diminishing**

The 6th on a scale:
A-B-C-D-E-**F**-G
Raised 6th:
A-B-C-D-E-**F#**-G

**Ascending / Descending**

Only changed when going up or down
## Essential scales
