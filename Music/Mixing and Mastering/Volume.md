It's down to taste

but here are some guidelines

**Target Numbers**
Initial levels should leave headroom for mixing and mastering
Peaks between -12 dbfs and -6 dbfs
Save your faders on the mixer for the final balancing stage.
****

**Instrument Heirarchy**
**The Anchors** ( Lead vocal, main melody instrument)
- Should be the loudest
**Bass and kick drum**
- Rhythmic core, should sit just underneath
**Supporters** (Rhythm and Pads)
- Rhythm instruments are there to fill the space and provide harmony.
- Acoustic guitars, piano, synth pads
- Their volume should be pulled back so they sit comfortably underneath the lead elements without fighting for attention
**The Highlights** ( ear candy )
- Background elements
- Arpeggios
- FX
- Much quieter in background, only acting as texture or ear candy
