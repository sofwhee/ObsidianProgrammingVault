Intro:
- Sets up the song's tempo.
- Key
- Mood
- Lasts about four bars

Verse:
- Develops the story or theme of the song
- Lyrics change with each verse

Pre-Chorus:
- A short section connecting the verse to the chorus
- Builds tension

Chorus:
- The main message and catchiest part
- Repeated throughout the song with consistent lyrics and melody

Bridge:
- A section providing a contrast to the rest of the song
- Often changes the tempo, key, or emotional feel

Outro:
- Concludes the song
- a fade out
- or final repeating chorus
